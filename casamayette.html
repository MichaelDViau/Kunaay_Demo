Property Highlights

Welcome to Casa Mayette, a stunning beachfront villa where turquoise waters and soft white sand set the stage for unforgettable memories. From the moment you arrive, the ocean is your constant view, just steps away from your private freshwater pool and sun loungers. This elegant home blends indoor comfort with outdoor living at its finest. Spend your days enjoying the ocean breeze, relaxing beneath the expansive grass palapa, or gathering around the handcrafted dining table for ten. Whether you’re swimming, sunbathing, entertaining, or simply unwinding, every detail is designed for relaxation. Inside, Casa Mayette features a newly remodeled gourmet kitchen, stylish new living room furnishings, and spacious dining areas perfect for family meals or evenings with friends. With its blend of modern elegance and coastal charm, this villa is the ideal setting to reconnect, recharge, and create lasting memories on the Riviera Maya.
